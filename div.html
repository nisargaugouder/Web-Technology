Sikandar Box Office Collection Day 8: Salman Khan's Film Crosses Rs 100 Crore In India


Salman Khan’s Sikandar is straight-up smashing it at the box office! On just day 8, the action-packed masala fest has officially crossed Rs 100 crore in India, and fans are loving it. After a thunderous opening weekend, the film has managed to keep the momentum going strong through the week.

According to the latest numbers, Sikandar added Rs 4.25 crore to its kitty on day 8, pushing its total India net collection to Rsc102 crore. The film saw a bit of a boost thanks to the weekend crowd trickling in, helping it finally cross that Rs 100 crore milestone. But here’s the tea: Sikandar opened at Rs 26 crore — not bad, but still trailing behind Chhaava, which roared in with Rs 31 crore on day one. By the end of its first week, Sikandar stood at Rs 90.25 crore.
